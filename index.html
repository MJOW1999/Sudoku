Michael's Sudoku

Instructions

  • Using the numbers 1-9 complete each 3x3 square of the board.
  • You cannot repeat a number in the same row, column or square.
  • You must not make 3 mistakes!
  • To win, you must solve the puzzle in the given time.
  • TIP: Try to use a process of elimination to solve the puzzle.
  • Have FUN!
